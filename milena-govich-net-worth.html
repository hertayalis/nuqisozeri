Milena Govich Net Worth

Milena Govich:

$4 million

Milena Govich is an American actress and singer. She started her career in the mid-2000s, first acting in short films. Her first notable role on the small screen was as Candy in Rescue Me. She then joined the main cast of the TV show Conviction as A.D.A. Jessica Rossi. At the same time, she became known for her role as Det. Nina Cassady in Law & Order. In the 2010s, she mostly guest-starred on some shows. This includes The Defenders, Body of Proof, Make It or Break It, and The Mentalist. Her latest acting work was as Lori Stevens in the show Finding Carter.
