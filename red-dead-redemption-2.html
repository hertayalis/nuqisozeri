Red Dead Redemption 2 Pig Mask Location

Pig Mask in RDR2 is one of several unique masks in the game. The Red Dead Redemption 2 unique Pig Mask can’t be bought in a store, like many others can. Instead, you have to find the RDR 2 pig mask location and take it for yourself. The only problem there is that the mask is off the beaten path, so you do have to go looking for it. So, here’s our Red Dead Redemption 2 Pig Mask Location guide to show you exactly where to find it.

Where to Find Pig Mask Location in RDR2?

To find the Unique Pig Mask in Red Dead Redemption 2, you’ll have to go to Butcher Creek. This location is northwest of the Van Horn Trading post, and southeast of Elysian Pool, in the south of the region called Roanoke Ridge. The place is just as lovely as it sounds. It’s basically a small gathering of “buildings”, and there’s flesh, blood, and guts literally everywhere. Approach the place where the butcher is standing and leaning on a pole. The RDR2 Pig Mask is hanging on a pole next to the one the dude is leaning on. When you approach it, you’ll get the prompt to switch your hat for the pig mask.

The mask should be in your horse menu once you pick it up. Mount your horse, and go into that menu. Go to the bottom left section, and use L2 and R2 to scroll between the items to find the pig mask. You can also just ride into the camp with the Pig Mask in your inventory. You’ll then be able to also find the mask in your Wardrobe inventory, and put it on whenever you want to look like a particularly gruesome serial killer. There are several other masks that make you look like a slasher movie villain, but the Pig Mask takes the cake.
