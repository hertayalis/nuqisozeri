Tyson Ritter Net Worth

Tyson Ritter is an American musician and actor born on April 24, 1984 in Stillwater, OK. He is best known as the lead singer and bassist of the alternative rock band The All-American Rejects, whose first hit song was "Swing, Swing". Ritter has been active in the music industry since 2001 and continues to make music today.
