Donald Trump Wins Iowa with Unprecedented Margin


Former US President Donald Trump secured an exceptional victory in the Iowa Republican caucuses on Monday night.

Kickstarting his presidential nomination bid, Trump easily triumphed in the first contest of the 2024 Republican presidential nominating calendar, surpassing anticipated results and breaking previous caucus records with over 50% of the vote.

Expressing gratitude in his caucus victory speech, Trump thanked the people of Iowa.

Florida Governor Ron DeSantis and former UN Ambassador Nikki Haley placed second and third respectively, each receiving around 20% of the vote.

Vivek Ramaswamy, who obtained approximately 8% support from caucus attendees, suspended his campaign and pledged his support to Trump.

In addition, the former President praised DeSantis, Haley, and biotech entrepreneur Ramaswamy for their performances, lauding them as “very smart” and “capable” individuals.

The Iowa results demonstrate the continued dedication of Republicans to Trump, despite his unconventional campaign conducted amidst ongoing legal battles and four indictments.
