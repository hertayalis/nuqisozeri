Rosario Dawson Net Worth

Rosario Dawson is an American actress born on May 9, 1979 in New York City, NY. She is best known for her roles in films such as Rent, Sin City, Seven Pounds, 25th Hour, Eagle Eye, Trance, Clerks II, Death Proof, Unstoppable, and Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief. In 2015, she began playing Claire Temple on the Netflix series Daredevil.
