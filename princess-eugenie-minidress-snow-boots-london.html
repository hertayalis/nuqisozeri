Princess Eugenie Sports Snow Boots &amp; Blue Zara Coat Out in London

Princess Eugenie is keeping busy as she awaits the birth of her first child.

The royal was photographed leaving work in London last week, wearing a casual, chic printed minidress; a blue wool Zara overcoat; opaque tights; fur-lined, chunky snow boots; and a protective face mask; she was also carrying a canvas work tote. Since Eugenie is not a senior working royal, she retains a day job as director of the city's Hauser & Wirth gallery, where she has worked for the last five years, per People.
