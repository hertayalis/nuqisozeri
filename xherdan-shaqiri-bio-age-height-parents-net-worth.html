Xherdan Shaqiri Biography

Xherdan Shaqiri is a professional Swiss footballer who plays for Switzerland National Team, and Chicago Fire as a Winger.

After earning a promotion to the Basel main team in 2009-2010, he stayed at the Swiss Super league side for 3 years. He made a total of 130 appearances and scored 23 goals for the club.

Xherdan later made a big change when he joined the German giants, Bayern Munich, in the Bundesliga in 2012-2013. The Swiss incredible player made 81 appearances with 17 goals to his tally in his 3 years stay in Munich.

He then joined the Italian Serie A when he joined Inter Milan in 2014-2015. His stay there was relatively brief as he managed to make 20 appearances with 3 goals to his numbers for the club.

The Swiss international changed leagues once again by joining the English Premier League(EPL) side, Stoke City, in 2014-2015. After enjoying 3 seasons there, he pulled off 92 appearances and scored 15 goals for the club.

He then joined the EPL Giants, Liverpool, in 2018-2019 where he managed he had a magnificent 3 seasons’ stay. He scored 63 goals with 8 goals to his name for the Merseyside club in all competitions before his departure.

The winger then said goodbye to the EPL and joined the French Ligue 1 for just a season. He joined Lyon in 2021-2022 and made 16 appearances with 2 goals to his name for the club.

Xherdan Shaqiri is currently having an incredible time in the Major League Soccer(MLS) after joining Chicago Fire in 2022. He has by far made a total of 63 appearances with 14 goals to his name for the United States club.
