Chloe x Halle Stun in Gold Louis Vuitton Gowns at the 2021 Grammys

Chloe x Halle are shining together at the 2021 Grammy Awards, even though the sisters are currently miles apart.

For music's biggest night, the R&B duo wore two coordinating gold gowns courtesy of Louis Vuitton. Chloe chose a floor length number that included a plunging sweetheart neckline, tiger stripe print and a thigh high leg slit. She finished the look with black mesh knit gloves and sexy black latex stiletto heeled boots. Halle opted for a shorter ensemble, sporting a thin strap-gold and black printed mini dress, a matching head wrap with a maxi length train, and black strappy heels. Chloe wore her hair half up in gorgeous bantu knots with flowing curls.

Louis Vuitton

Though the pair was nominated for three awards this evening, they didn't take any home. The sisters graciously accepted the loss while speaking with E!'s Giuliana Rancic during the award ceremony's pre-show. Chloe x Halle's 2020 album Ungodly Hour was nominated for Best Progressive R&B Album, their song "Wonder What She Thinks of Me" for Best Traditional R&B performance, and their hit "Do It" was also nominated for Best R&B Song.

"I'm filled with such gratitude," Chloe told Rancic regarding their loss this evening. "I'm missing my sister Halle so, so much. We found out that we didn't win the three awards tonight but it's OK, we're going to get them next time. I'm just happy that we were able to put so much love into the music that we created and can't wait for what's next."
