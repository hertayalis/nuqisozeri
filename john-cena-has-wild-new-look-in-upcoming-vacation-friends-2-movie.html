John Cena Has Wild New Look In Upcoming Vacation Friends 2 Movie

John Cena is one of the biggest names in professional wrestling, as he was the face of WWE for well over a decade. He is now a part-timer, having a busy schedule in Hollywood. That said, it appears he has a wild new look for the upcoming ‘Vacation Friends 2’ movie.

The Leader Of Cenation never fails to give it his best no matter what role he is given and his Hollywood accomplishments in the past few years speak for themselves. This includes his roles in films such as ‘The Suicide Squad’ and ‘Peacemaker’ and even the ‘Fast & Furious’ movies.

A photo from the set of Vacation Friends 2 has been making rounds on social media, which showed John Cena in a completely different look from what fans have expected from him.

John Cena also returned during the WWE Money In The Bank Premium Live event. We will have to wait and see when The Leader of Cenation will be making his return to WWE television once again.
