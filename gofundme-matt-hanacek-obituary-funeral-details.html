GoFundMe; Matt Hanacek Obituary, Funeral Details

Sharing the news of our cousin Matt’s unexpected and tragic death on December 13th is something we do with heavy hearts and deep regret.

His beloved wife, Jess, and their three lovely girls are front and center in our hearts as we try to make sense of this tragic loss, especially when Christmas is just two weeks away.

Even though we didn’t cross paths too often, Matt’s love for Jess and their three girls had no bounds.

Everyone he met could feel the unwavering commitment he had as a husband and father, and he left an unforgettable impression on their lives. Matt’s affection for his loved ones was limitless, and he devoted his life to making sure they had happiness, laughter, and unforgettable experiences. We feel obligated to gather around Jess and the girls at this time of deep sorrow, providing comfort and support as they face the difficult road ahead. An unfathomable tragedy has cast a pall over the holiday season, which was supposed to be a season of joy and togetherness.

We, the people they call “family and friends,” need to band together to help them out financially. In our efforts to remember Matt, let us reach out to Jess and the daughters, offering our support to ease their immediate and future worries. Amid a season intended for joy and togetherness, no family should have to go through such a devastating loss. We respectfully request your assistance in any manner you see fit if you are moved to contribute. Your kind contributions will let Jess and the daughters know that they are not going through this tough time alone.
