Shoppers Walked 10 Miles in These $53 Cloud-Like Spring Sneakers

We spend a majority of time on our feet, so it’s practically essential to have a pair of comfortable shoes on hand. In other words, there’s a time and a place for strappy heels and stiletto boots, but it’s always a good idea to reach for cushiony kicks when you’re getting in those steps. The latest sneaker on my radar? The on-sale Somiliss tennis shoe.

The highly-rated sneaker is on sale for $53 for a limited time and comes in sizes 5 to 9.5 as well as various colors, like beige and green, both perfect for spring. But that’s not even the best part: A chunky, non-slip sole, lightweight build, lace-up closure, and soft interior deliver an out-of-this-world comfort shoe that’s both stylish and functional. Even better, the upper is made from a quick-drying cloth, suede, and leather to prevent sweaty feet, keeping you dry all day long. 

Somiliss Non-Slip Sneaker

What’s more, the sneakers also resemble Jennifer Aniston and Meghan Markle’s go-to New Balance kicks, and even look a lot like these on-sale Steve Madden Campo Sneakers. At a major price reduction, the Somiliss has a similar retro aesthetic, channeling a “super expressive” look at an “affordable price,” according to one Amazon shopper.

The shoes don’t only look good, but feel good, too. One reviewer was very impressed, noting the sneakers have “so much cushion, it’s like walking on a cloud.” In fact, they added that they’re getting a second color to complete their collection. Someone else walked 10 miles in the Somiliss tennis shoes and didn’t have sore feet afterwards — and that’s something to praise. To put it simply, they’re “worth your money,” per another reviewer.

Somiliss Non-Slip Sneaker

Styling the Somiliss sneakers is simple, especially when they feature a neutral color palette and sleek build. Wear them with yoga pants, a tank top, and baseball cap for a road trip, straight-leg jeans and a T-shirt for a casual picnic, or leggings and a sports bra for the gym. You can even dress them up for work with a blazer, wide-leg trousers, and some jewelry. Sneakers are the epitome of street style, so no matter how you wear them, you’ll look effortlessly chic and so cool.   

With over 400 people buying the Somiliss sneaker in the past month, I doubt they’ll stay in stock for long — especially when there’s a pretty sweet discount available. So, secure your favorite color (or both, while you’re at it!) before the price goes back up.
