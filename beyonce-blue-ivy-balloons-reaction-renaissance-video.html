Watch Beyonc and Blue Ivy React to Fans Bringing Balloons for Blues Dance

Blue Ivy Carter has been nailing it onstage with mom Beyoncé amid the singer’s ongoing Renaissance World Tour—and per new video footage from her most recent performance, she seems just as starstruck by her growing base of fans.

During the tour stop in Germany last night, Bey and Blue were captured beaming together onstage upon receiving blue balloons from the crowd following their now much-awaited mother-daughter performance. In a fan-captured video, Blue Ivy is seen looking out in pure awe at thousands of cheering fans, as Beyoncé smiles at her eldest and shouts to the crowd, “Give it up for my beautiful Blue!”

As in their other joint performances from the tour, the duo coordinated in matching silver ensembles from Loewe for the routine.

Blue Ivy made her first surprise appearance on the tour last month in Paris, where she joined her mom onstage to perform dance routines to selections from The Gift, Bey’s soundtrack for 2019’s remake of The Lion King. During her performance of “My Power,” Beyoncé asked the crowd to “give it up for Blue,” at which point the 11-year-old suddenly arrived onstage and danced like a natural for the rest of the track.

Since then, the singer’s oldest child has joined her in additional performances on the European leg of the tour, going viral in fan-captured clips that have since garnered Blue a growing fan base dubbed the “Ivy League.” (Members include younger sister Rumi Carter.)

Following her daughter’s initial world-shaking performance in Paris, Bey shared a loving tribute to Blue on Instagram, expressing her love and pride after the showstopping cameo. “My beautiful first born 🙏🏾 I’m so proud and thankful to be your mama,” the singer wrote alongside a photo of her daughter in action onstage. “You bring us so much joy, my sweet angel.”
